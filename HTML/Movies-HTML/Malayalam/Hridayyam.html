Hridayyam

Hridiya is a 2022 Malayalam language romantic film written and directed by Vineeth Srinivasan . Kalyani Priyadarshan is the heroine in this film starring Pranav Mohanlal . This is Pranav Mohanlal's third film after Aadhi and Bhishiyanaam Sandhyam. Another special feature of this film is that Maryland Cinemas, a leading film production company in South India, is returning after forty years. Vysakh Subramanian is producing this film for Maryland. Newcomer Hisham Abdul Wahab is composing the music for this film.
