Vadakupatty Ramasamy

Vadakkupatti Ramasamy is a 2024 Indian Tamil-language period comedy drama film written and directed by Karthik Yogi of Dikkiloona fame and starring Santhanam and Megha Akash.A selfish, money-minded charlatan makes a living off his fellow villagers using their superstitious beliefs. When his major source of income is shut down, he tries numerous ways to reopen the temple.
